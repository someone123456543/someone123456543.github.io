How to find time in a noisy world

Read time: don´t worry! It won´t take long :)

Do you also feel overwhelmed by your daily life?

Here is some nice things I'd like to share with you

I hope it lightens your day <3

First things first, let's talk about the elephant in the room: urban daily life and expectations. When I think about the word "stress" what usually comes to mind is traffic, public transport, getting to work, exiting work and doing it all over again. Meeting deadlines and expectations can also be somewhat emotionally taxing. The truth is it feels like there's a lot of urgency nowadays to get somewhere, to achieve something or to be in a certain way. I sometimes question myself: why? What is the big shiny thing we are trying to get? Some of us just want to survive, or sometimes we want fame, sometimes luxury. I believe it is okay to want to treat ourselves and have nice things. The problem lies, the way i see it, on the reason behind that and what exactly it means to treat ourselves, to properly take care of ourselves. Consuming becomes a problem when it attempts to replace true joy, when we mistake pleasure for joy. I guess sometimes we are taught by people who haven't figured that out themselves. It's okay. We can always relearn and connect with who we are.

"We are all leaves of one tree. We are all waves of one sea" - Thich Nhat Hanh

Now, enough of philosophy.

In this website you can find some Jack Johnson music (no, i wasn't paid to advertise). I really enjoy some of his songs and hope you will too. I also shared a breathing exercise youtube video in case you give meditation a shot. There are different types of meditation which you can try. The link to the video is in the box "Meditation for Beginners" and it only takes five minutes for the ones that are in a rush. You can pretty much do it anywhere. I, for example, do it during my commute (I use public transport). I'm interested in hearing your story, so if you feel like sharing it scroll down and you'll find a box where you can write it.
